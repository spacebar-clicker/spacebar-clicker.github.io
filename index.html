HOW TO PLAY SNOW RIDER 3D

  • Move: Use the arrow keys or W, A, S, D to steer your sleigh.
  • Jump: Press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles.
  • Collect Gifts: Try to pick up gifts along the way for extra points.
  • Avoid Obstacles: Steer clear of trees, rocks, and other hazards on the slopes.

Stay focused, react quickly, and enjoy the snowy thrill as you race down the mountain!
