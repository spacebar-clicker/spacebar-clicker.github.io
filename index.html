HOW TO PLAY SHELL SHOCKERS

  • Move: Use the W, A, S, D keys to navigate around the map.
  • Shoot: Click the Left Mouse Button to fire your weapon.
  • Aim: Use the Mouse to aim at enemies.
  • Reload: Press R to reload your weapon.
  • Jump: Press the Spacebar to jump.
  • Switch Weapon: Press Q to switch between weapons.
  • Special Abilities: Use E to activate any special abilities, depending on the character.

Take cover, aim accurately, and watch out for opponents as you battle it out in this egg-citing multiplayer game!
